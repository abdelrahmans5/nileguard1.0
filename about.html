About NileGuard

Our Project

NileGuard is an innovative project dedicated to protecting the Nile River and the Egyptian environment through data analysis and sustainable solutions.

We leverage World Bank data to understand current environmental challenges and develop effective strategies to reduce pollution and water scarcity.
